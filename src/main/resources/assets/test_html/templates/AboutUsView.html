TeachOversea

A full-circle career support system for teachers

seeking employment abroad

We aim to source talent and opportunity and intelligently connect the two, while offering high-quality customer service from start to finish.

Who We Are

TeachOversea was built by a group of passionate bridge-builders. We are excited to help job-seekers and employers connect. We greatly respect those who are willing to take on the challenge of the expat-life, and we want to make the daunting task of finding international employment as straight-forward as possible.

What We Offer

  • An online portal for both recruiters and candidates.

    Both sides are instantly show a selection of jobs or candidates that match their requirements on a sliding scale.

  • Smart matching algorithm

    Both sides can determine whether a job or candidate is a good fit without blindly applying to multiple jobs with no feedback.
